My Story

Why four domains?

Most engineers pick a specialization early and go deep. I went wide first — deliberately. I believe the most interesting engineering problems live at the intersections.

A firmware engineer who understands VLSI makes better hardware-software co-design decisions. An ML engineer who writes embedded C deploys models that actually run on target hardware. A full-stack developer who understands circuit-level systems builds better IoT platforms.

That's the engineer I'm building myself into — one who can move fluidly from the RTL level to the cloud API layer, from silicon tape-out to user interface.
